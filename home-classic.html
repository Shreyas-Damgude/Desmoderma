PRP Treatment

PRP treatment for hair loss is a complete package where the patient needs to undergo 4-6 consecutive and spaced-out treatments to experience the results they are looking for

Hair Transplant

A hair transplant is a procedure in which a plastic or dermatological surgeon moves hair to a bald area of the head. The surgeon usually moves hair from the back or side of the head to the front or top of the head.

LHR Treatment

LHR is laser hair REDUCTION and NOT REMOVAL- This means that after the laser hair gets reduced in number and become thin rather than completely disappearing
