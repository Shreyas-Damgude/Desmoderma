Hair Transplant

A hair transplant is a procedure in which a plastic or dermatological surgeon moves hair to a bald area of the head. The surgeon usually moves hair from the back or side of the head to the front or top of the head.

LHR Treatment

LHR is laser hair REDUCTION and NOT REMOVAL- This means that after the laser hair gets reduced in number and become thin rather than completely disappearing

Principal Of Hair Transplantation

“Hair transplantation surgery is based on the principal of ‘Donor Dominance’. Hair at he back and side continue to grow throughout the life of a person, even in a man with severe hair loss. These hair are resistant to the hormonal influence. When the hair is transplanted in bald area, it continues to grow naturally and can be washed, trimmed and styled as one wants to.”
